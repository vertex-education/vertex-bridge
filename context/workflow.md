# Current vs. Future Workflow — VSS SFO Client Onboarding

## Current (Broken) Workflow
1. Sales closes deal in HubSpot
2. Onboarding project manually created in Asana and assigned to client
3. Client receives Asana invite — must create/log in to Asana account
4. Client sees wall of 100+ undifferentiated tasks (not filtered for their state/status)
5. Client uploads docs, completes tasks with no guidance on why or how
6. Internal teams monitor Asana across interconnected tasks to find relevant data
7. Mistakes discovered after submission → rework loops → missed deadlines
8. Manual back-and-forth to fix errors, chase missing info

**Pain points:**
- Asana not designed as a client-facing onboarding tool
- No personalization — every client sees every task
- No AI guidance — clients confused, make mistakes
- Internal teams play detective to find their data
- High rework rate, deadline pressure

---

## Future (Portal) Workflow
1. Deal closes in HubSpot — contract and contact data captured
2. **Portal automatically provisions client login** (username + temp password or magic link)
   - Based on primary contact in HubSpot
   - No manual setup required from Vertex team
3. Vertex delivers portal link to client (via automated email, Sales rep, team leader — flexible)
4. Client clicks link → logs in immediately → sees personalized dashboard
   - Name and school pre-populated
   - Tasks filtered by: state, new vs. existing, contracted services (SFO/SPA/Additional)
   - URGENT tasks surfaced first
5. Client works through wizard step-by-step:
   - AI agent explains each step and why it matters
   - Client can narrate/describe info in plain language → AI transforms to structured data
   - AI reviews submissions for completeness and format before finalizing
   - Client prompted to fix issues before submission (not after)
6. Completed submissions automatically routed to correct Vertex internal team by Functional Area
7. Internal teams receive organized, pre-validated data — no detective work

**Key improvements:**
- One place, personalized from day one
- No re-entry of data Vertex already has
- AI guidance reduces confusion and errors
- Automatic routing eliminates internal sorting work
- Rework dramatically reduced — issues caught before submission
- Professional, modern experience that reflects well on Vertex

---

## HubSpot Data Used for Personalization
| HubSpot Field | Portal Use |
|---|---|
| Primary contact name/email | Pre-populates client greeting, sets up login |
| State | Filters state-specific tasks |
| Contracted services (SFO/SPA/Additional) | Shows only relevant functional areas |
| New vs. Existing school | Shows correct task variants |
| Contract start date | Sets onboarding deadline/urgency |

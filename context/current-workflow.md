# Current Workflow Context

## Current Problem Pattern

VSS charter school clients currently navigate onboarding through fragmented tasks, documents, and communication channels. The experience is not always tailored to the specific services they purchased. Vertex internal teams may need to manually track submissions across Asana, file folders, emails, and other tools.

## Current Pain Points

- Clients may not know which onboarding tasks apply to them.
- Clients may not understand why documents are needed.
- Tasks and files may live in different systems.
- Vertex teams may need to manually monitor Asana and file submissions.
- Sensitive documents require careful handling.
- Internal teams need timely visibility into client progress.

## Improved Demo Workflow

1. Admin sends a school invite email.
2. Jack creates a real account.
3. Jack enters the School Onboarding portal.
4. Portal reads SFO tasks from Asana.
5. Jack sees a guided journey sorted by due date.
6. Jack can ask the AI helper questions.
7. Jack uploads a file for a task.
8. The app stores the file in R2.
9. The matching Asana task is completed.
10. Vertex Dashboard shows progress.

## Key Workflow Principle

The portal is the client front door. Asana remains the internal workflow system.

## Key Insights for Wizard Design

1. **State matters a lot** — many tasks are state-specific (NV, CA, NY, PA, TN etc.)
2. **New vs Existing distinction** — the wizard must know whether the school is brand new or transitioning from another provider
3. **Functional areas map to Vertex internal teams** — tasks can be auto-routed by FA tag
4. **URGENT tasks** (Payroll) should surface first or be highlighted as must-complete early
5. **117 total tasks** — far too many to show all at once; the wizard must filter intelligently by: state, new vs existing, and contracted services
6. **Some tasks are clearly for Vertex staff** (e.g., "Notify our PDE contacts") — the wizard should only show client-facing tasks

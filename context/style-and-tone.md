# Style and Tone Context

## Product Tone

The portal should feel professional, calm, and client-service oriented. The client should feel guided, not overwhelmed.

## Writing Principles

1. Use plain language.
2. Avoid internal jargon unless it is explained.
3. Keep instructions short and actionable.
4. Reassure the client when they complete a step.
5. Avoid sounding robotic or overly technical.
6. Avoid legal, financial, payroll, tax, or compliance advice.

## Sample Confirmation Copy

Thanks, Jack. Your file has been uploaded for Heritage Summit Schools, and this onboarding step has been marked complete.

## Sample AI Copy

Jack, this step is asking for documentation that helps Vertex confirm the official business identity for Heritage Summit Schools. If you do not have a 501c3 letter, the task instructions may allow EIN or official business-name documentation instead.

## Error Message Style

Be clear and reassuring.

Example:

We could not complete that upload just now. Your task has not been marked complete yet. Please try again, or contact your Vertex onboarding team if the issue continues.
